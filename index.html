Minesweeper

Julia Kozak - Feb 2022

It's Minesweeper made in P5.js! This is a 13x13 board with 30 mines. Right click to reveal, left click to flag, center click to chord. You may lose on the first move (the board is pre-loaded) so reload if you win/lose.
